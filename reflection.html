What do I want to learn more about?

FUTURE INQUIRY

I've been closely following the development of AI. After this period of research and switching between different AI systems, I can say that AI has undeniably demonstrated its potential to improve productivity. With AI, most people can be more efficient or accomplish things they couldn't do before. There are two questions I want to clarify. First, how will AI become a truly locally deployed assistant in the future? Will it be through an increase in the quantity of hardware, an improvement in computing quality, or a new computing paradigm that allows for the miniaturization and localization of AI agents? Currently, all AI is limited by computing power and is accessed online. Second, given such a significant change, how can we adapt our learning methods and educational models to this shift in "tools"?
